## What is it?

The Collatz Conjecture can be defined simply as:

$$
F(x) = 
     \begin{cases}
       \dfrac{n}{2} &\text{if }{n} \equiv 0 \\
       3{n}+1 &\text{if }{n} \equiv 1 \\
     \end{cases}
$$

With any number being passed in recursivly converging on 1 (apparently).
